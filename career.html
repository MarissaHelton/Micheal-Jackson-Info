Early Career

Michael Jackson began his career at a very young age as part of the Jackson 5. His talent was apparent early on, and he quickly became the lead singer of the group.

The Jackson 5 achieved great success with hits like "I Want You Back" and "ABC," propelling Michael into the spotlight.

Solo Career

After leaving the Jackson 5, Michael Jackson launched a groundbreaking solo career. Albums like *Off the Wall* and *Thriller* changed the music landscape forever.

His innovative music videos, including the iconic "Thriller" video, revolutionized the industry, and his influence continues to be felt today.
